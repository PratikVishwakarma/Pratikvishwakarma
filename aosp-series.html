A structured deep-dive series for engineers who want to understand the framework beneath the APIs — not just how to call them, but why they exist and what they do internally.

Every phase follows one rule: WHY before HOW. The motivation for a system is explained before its mechanics. An engineer who understands why Binder exists will debug a transaction failure faster than one who has only memorised the API surface.
