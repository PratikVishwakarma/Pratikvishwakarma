about

Building what runs beneath the app.

I'm an Android Platform Engineer with 8+ years of experience. My first five years were in product — shipping 20+ mobile apps end to end across healthcare, gaming, social, and community domains. The last three have been deep in the platform layer: AOSP system apps, WindowManager overlays, Binder/IPC, and TV/STB platform engineering on-site with a major telecom client.

I believe in understanding the WHY before the HOW. Most Android engineers know how to call an API. I'm interested in what happens inside it — how Binder serialises a transaction, how WindowManager decides focus, how a HAL call crosses the kernel boundary.

Currently focused on building deep expertise in AOSP framework layers, writing publicly about internals for the broader Android community, and exploring Android Automotive as a next domain frontier.
